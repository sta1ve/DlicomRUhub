What is Dlicom
01

Social Network

Publish posts, chat in encrypted messages, earn tips in DLI tokens. A censorship-resistant alternative to traditional social networks.

02

Crypto Wallet

Self-custody wallet with crypto and NFT support. Best-rate swaps, pay gas fees with DLI tokens, send directly in chat.

03

DeFi & Staking

Stake DLI tokens for USDT rewards. Multiple periods available. Access dApps via built-in Web3 browser.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Junior Hunter role?+
Fill out the application form and get the Junior Hunter role — it grants access to #nominations and other private channels during the Hunt.
What is the difference between Regional Lead and Regional Helper?+
Regional Lead is the community manager of their region: growth, retention, campaigns, analytics. Regional Helper is the leader's assistant: answers questions, guides members, runs events, keeps the chat active.
How to become Regional Lead?+
1. Fill out the application form. 2. In Discord, choose your region. 3. Actively participate in all three phases (27 Feb — 27 Mar).
Is Dliever required to join the Regional Hunt?+
No. Dliever is not required to apply. But active participants earn it automatically and have priority in selection.
What is the DLI token?+
DLI is Dlicom's native token on Ethereum. Used for: sending crypto in chat, tips, gas payments, staking with USDT rewards, and voting.
Is KYC required?+
No. Dlicom is a fully decentralized project operating as a DEX. No KYC required. Profiles are based on Ethereum addresses.
What is the Dili mascot contest?+
Create a story, lore or concept about Dili (text, video, art). The best 10 ideas earn the Dliever role. Publish in #creators, quoting the X post.
How to earn on the platform?+
Staking — DLI for USDT rewards. Content — tips from followers. Referrals — invite via your code.
Can I apply for Regional Lead directly, skipping Helper?+
Yes. By applying for Regional Lead, you automatically become a candidate for Regional Helper as well.
Why is now the best time to join?+
It's the earliest stage — no moderators, no leads in regions yet. Starting from zero right now is the best window to become a Regional Lead or Helper.
